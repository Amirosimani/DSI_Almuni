About Us

COLUMBIA UNIVERSITY
DATA SCIENCE INSTITUTE

The Data Science Institute at Columbia University was designed to connect and train the world's leading data scientists. Consisting of faculty and students across a wide range of fields, it has two educational programs - a Master of Science in Data Science and a Certification of Professional Achievement in Data Sciences.

DATA SCIENCE INSTITUTE
ALUMNI NETWORK

The Data Science Institute Alumni network was founded by DSI graduates to foster a lifelong connection among alumni, our alma mater, and the global data science community. Through social and networking events, we hope to afford students and alumni of DSI the chance to learn and benefit from each other.
